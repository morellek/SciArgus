#Wild boar and African Swine Fever
Research on ungulate species, particularly wild boar, investigating how movement patterns and social interactions shape their response to environmental and anthropogenic factors, especially in relation to the spread of infectious diseases such as African swine fever (ASF).

#Wildlife tracking technologies
alternative tracking technologies to traditional collars to enhance animal welfare and improve wildlife monitoring. 

#Movement ecology
spatial ecology, biologging, and wildlife tracking technologies applied to disease ecology.
